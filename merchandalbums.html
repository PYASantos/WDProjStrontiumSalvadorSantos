Lightstick

The official lightstick for ZEROBASEONE is designed as a symbol for their fandom, ZEROSE, and the groups foundational concept. The head of the lightstick is shaped like a crystal flower or rose, specifically referencing the blue rose which serves as the fandoms official symbol. This clear, glass-like design symbolizes the groups glorious beginning, and notably features the digital display 0_1 inside the bulb, which directly represents their name, ZEROBASEONE (0, _ for base, and 1). As a concert accessory, it can synchronize with their music via Bluetooth during live performances.
