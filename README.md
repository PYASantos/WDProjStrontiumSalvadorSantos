## **Comprehensive Description**
ZB1 Zone is a website dedicated to the K-pop group **ZEROBASEONE (ZB1)**, created to help new and existing fans learn more about the group and their journey. The website offers a detailed look into the members’ profiles, their formation through *Boys Planet*, their music releases, and official merchandise.  

To make the experience interactive, visitors can also take a fun personality quiz to discover which member they’re most similar to—helping them find their “bias” in the group. **ZB1 Zone** serves as both an informative hub and a fun fan space for everything related to ZEROBASEONE.

---

## **Outline of Pages**

### **1. Homepage**
Contains the main navigation menu leading to all other pages and a brief description of who ZEROBASEONE is. It introduces the site’s purpose and highlights the group’s rise in K-pop.

### **2. Members**
Showcases photos and profiles of each member, including personal details, positions, company, and fun facts. It will also feature their rankings and number of votes during the *Boys Planet* finale.

### **3. Boys Planet**
Provides background information about *Boys Planet*, the reality survival show that formed ZB1. Includes the show’s mechanics, notable performances, and clips from the finale.

### **4. Music**
Displays the group’s discography with album covers, tracklists, song meanings, and music/dance practice videos. Includes streaming service links where fans can listen to their songs.

### **5. Merch & Albums**
Shows both official and fan-made merchandise, describing what each item includes and where it can be purchased. Includes links to official stores and trusted retailers.

### **6. Quiz: Find Your ZB1 Match**
An interactive quiz page where users answer questions about their personality and preferences to discover which ZB1 member they are most similar to. The result includes an image and short description of the member.

---

## **JavaScript Incorporation**
JavaScript will be used on the **Quiz page** to handle question navigation, process user inputs, and dynamically generate the personalized results. It will display the user’s ZB1 match in real-time without reloading the page.  

Additional JavaScript features will include:
- **Hover animations** on the Members page to reveal fun facts.  
- **Image slideshow** on the Homepage showcasing ZB1 photos and updates.
