Boys Planet

Information

Boys Planet (보이즈 플래닛) is a survival show created by Mnet. A male version of Girls Planet 999, it premiered on February 2, 2023. The show aired every Thursday at 8:50 PM (KST) with 12 episodes in total. It ended on April 20 with the announcement of the nine members of the boy group ZEROBASEONE.
